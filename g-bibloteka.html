Jak vypujčyr knygu?

Aby vypujčyr knygu, treba vypelnyr forma. V otskazy otrymaš imejla z odpovednym plykem.
Vazne: Museš mar konta v bibloteky. Jesly jego nemaš, zalozyi za pomocom forma

Jak dar knygu?

Aby dar knygu treba vyslar na imejla gnezenskabibloteka@wp.pl plyk z ibukem lub plyky z fotografamy stron knyg. Take sa vymagana z tym:

  • Muset byla cela knyga, ne jej čustka
  • V fotografah knyg musa byr strony (muset tez byt okladka i numery stron)
  • Vsy fotografy musa byr čytelne
  • Prekazane knygy treba zarezervovar pres forma. Zanym to zdelaš, spravdi v ponyskojovym katalogym, uz ktos zarezervoval. Rezervaca trvat pres 2 sudmdeny.

    How to lend a book?

    To lend a book, you have to fill the form. In answer you receive e-mail with a suitable plik.
    Important: You must have account in library. If you don't have, create it with form

    How to give a book?

    If you want to give a book, you have to write to e-mail of the library gnezenskabibloteka@wp.pl file with e-book or files with photos of pages of the book. We have these requirements:

    • It has to be all the book, not the part.
    • The files with photos of book have to have the photos of cover. It have to be the numbers of pages on the photos.
    • All the photos have to be readable.
    • Giving a book you have to book by a form. Before you'll do this, check in catalogue below, has someone already booked. Reservation is during 2 weeks.
